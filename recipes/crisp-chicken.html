Crisp Chicken Recipe

This is a recipe for deliciously crispy baked chicken. The coating of the chicken is made of crushed potato chips which provides a lovely crispy skin. Also, this chicken is baked so you get that great cruncy exterior with very juciy and flavourful chicken. Added bonus of it being a healthier option to frying, saving the calories but not sacrafising flavour!

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken breast/thigh
  • Garlic butter (normal butter will work also)
  • large bag of potato chips, I use the standard salted flavour but you can use any flavour you like!
  • Seasoning mix of your choise (I use the Nandos Garlic Peri Peri Rub)/li>
  • Block of mild chedar cheese

Steps

  1. Wash chicken and place in a container. Generously covery your chicken with season mix. Once covered leave in fridy for at least 2 hours to marinade
  2. Make a small opening in potato chip bag, let air out and crush chips to a rough ground
  3. In a bowl, empty in your crushed chips and add 1 cup of graded chedar cheese
  4. In a seperate bowl, add the following seasonings: 1tsp cayane pepper, 1 tsp garlic poweder, 1tsp onion powder, 1 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  5. Add season mix to chips and cheese and combine in a bowl
  6. Use a stick of butter to grease base and sides of the baking container
  7. Place chicken inside baking dish, then cover with the chips/cheese mixture. Make sure chicken is completely covered
  8. Melt 1/4 cup of garlic butter and pour over the top
  9. Preheat over to 200 degrees C
  10. Bake chicken for 30 mins or until chicken is fully cooked through
  11. This dish is great served with mashed potatoes and gravy
