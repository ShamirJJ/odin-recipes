Peanut Butter Reeces Pieces Cookies

This is a recipe for soft, chewey, peanut buttery cookies packed with Reeces Pieces candies. Perfect for any peanut butter lover!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter (smooth - skippy recommended!)
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspon vanilla extract
  • 1 and 3/4 cups of plain flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teamspoon of salt
  • 1 cup Reeces Pieces candy (optional, these can be swapped for peanut butter chips also!)
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chps

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together butter, peanut butter and sugars until light and fluffy
  2. Beat in the egg
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract
  4. In another bowl, mix the flour, baking soda and salt. Gradually add to wet mix until well combined
  5. Stir in the Reeces Pieces (or peanut butter chips) and choclate chips
  6. Chill the cookie dough for at least 30 minutes
  7. Preheat over to 350 degrees F/175 degrees C and line a baking tray with parchment paper
  8. Roll balls of cookie dough together and place on tray leaving two inches between cookie balls
  9. Bake cookies for 10-12 minutes
  10. Once finished cooking, place cookies on a wire rack to cool for 5 minutes
  11. Enjoy!
